Voting Rules

  • Each person is allowed only one vote.
  • Voters must meet eligibility criteria (e.g., age, registration).
  • Ensure the ballot is filled out correctly within the designated area.
  • Votes must be cast before the deadline.
  • Voting is confidential to protect your privacy.
  • Double voting or fraud is strictly prohibited.
