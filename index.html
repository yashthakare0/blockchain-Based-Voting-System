               STEPS FOR ONLINE VOTING

Login to Website

Logging into a blockchain-based voting system website typically involves a secure authentication process to ensure the integrity of the voting process. Once logged in, users can access the voting interface, where they can: - View Voting Options: Review available voting options, including candidates or proposals. - Cast Votes: Submit their votes securely through the blockchain network. - Track Voting Status: Monitor the voting process and view real-time results.

Cast Your Vote

- Authentication: Once registered, you'll be authenticated through a secure process, which may include fingerprint recognition, facial recognition, or a one-time password ¹. - Voting: After authentication, you can cast your vote by selecting your preferred candidate or option. Your vote will be recorded on the blockchain, ensuring its security and transparency ¹ ².

Verify Your Vote

Verification Steps: Option 1: Transaction ID (TXID) 1. Check your email or voting platform dashboard for TXID. 2. Visit the blockchain explorer website (e.g., Etherscan, (link unavailable)). 3. Enter TXID in the search bar. 4. Verify vote details (e.g., candidate, timestamp). Option 2: Voter ID 1. Log in to the voting platform. 2. Go to "My Votes" or "Vote History." 3. Enter Voter ID or password. 4. Verify vote details.
